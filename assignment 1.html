    1.   Find the distance between the following pairs of points:

        (i)   (2,3),(4,1)
        (ii)  (-5,7),(-1,3)
        (iii) (a,b),(-a,-b)

                given by,
                (x1-x2)2 + (y1-y2)2

                Distance = (2-4)2 + (3-1)2 = (-2)2+(2)2 = 4 + 4 = 8
